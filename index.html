The idea

Not a menu. A table.

In Georgia, a meal is never just food — it's a supra: a long table, a toastmaster, and course after course arriving until midnight. We built this place to hold that tradition in the middle of the city. Everything is made the way it's made at home — the dough pulled by hand, the wine poured from clay, the toasts unhurried.

"Let this toast be to the table itself — to those who filled it before us, and to those still on their way."

— a Georgian toast, as old as the vine
